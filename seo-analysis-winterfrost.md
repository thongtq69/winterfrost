## üö® COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Keyword Stuffing**
   - Keep keyword density natural (1-2%)
   - Focus on user intent, not just keywords

2. **Thin Content**
   - Minimum 800 words per service page
   - Quality over quantity for blog posts

3. **Neglecting Mobile**
   - Test everything on mobile first
   - Mobile speed is critical

4. **Ignoring Analytics**
   - Setup proper tracking from day 1
   - Review data weekly

5. **Black Hat SEO**
   - No bought links
   - No keyword stuffing
   - No cloaking or hidden text

6. **Inconsistent NAP**
   - Same business info everywhere
   - Regular citation audits

7. **Slow Implementation**
   - SEO takes time but action is immediate
   - Don't wait for perfection

8. **Not Tracking Competitors**
   - Monitor competitor rankings monthly
   - Steal good ideas, improve them
